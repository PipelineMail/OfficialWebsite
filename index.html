The Problem Of Traditional Mailboxes

In the past few decades, the mail system has brought us great convenience, but it also has many problems.

01. Provide personal information

When you sign up for X-mail, you have to provide personal information to the X-mail operators. In many cases, this information
can be used to determine who you are.

03. Your mail is being monitored

Mailboxes operators have been providing your mail to governments. Believe it or not, your email has been monitored in the past and will be monitored in the future. It's common sense.

05. Near real time transmission

Emails don't always come on time due to different time zones and so on. If I need to receive a captcha by mail, this may result
in me always receiving an expired captcha.

02. Plaintext transmission, no privacy

Emails are sent in plain text without encryption. Although some mailboxes offer the option to send encrypted emails, that's just psychological comfort.In fact, you don't have any privacy.

04. Spam flood

Since email is basically free to send, anyone who knows your email account can send email to you. This has led to a flood of advertising, promotional and other spam.

06. Attachment size is limited

Attachments sent with mail are generally limited in size.

What kind of mail system do we need?

First, users' privacy must be protected.

Second, users provide little or no personal information to the platform.

Third, users are protected from spam.

 

PipeMail solves these problems.

What are the advantages of PipeMail?

If you want to use PipeMail, all you need to do is connect to your digital wallet, nothing else needs to be provided.

No Registration Required

Mail is encrypted with user's public-key. Everyone else, including PipeMail can't get raw mail data, only encrypted data.

User-controlled Encryption

Only the sender and recipient can decrypt the mail, no one can monitor your mail.
Your privacy can be completely protected.

Privacy Protection

PipeMail was developed using Solidity, which means PipeMail can run on any machine/chain that can run Solidity code.

Good Compatibility & Portability

With PipeMail, you can send digital assets along with your mail.
 
 
 

Send Token & NFT

To send a mail using PipeMail, the sender needs to buy stamps and pay for gas. This greatly reduces the amount of spam.

Almost no spam
